What type of students are in FCCLA?

FCCLA is comprised of responsible, respectful, and caring students who are investing their time to make a difference in their lives, and the lives of others. Most FCCLA students go on to be successful people, taking part in the career that they find most enjoyable!

Hey, what if I don't know what I want to do?

FCCLA has many opportunities, and everyone is welcome anywhere and in any group. Members are encouraged to find their niche so they can excel. FCCLA wants everyone to feel included and to have a role.

Does FCCLA help students in High School?

Confidence. FCCLA helps increase confidence in students, and inclines to make everyone included. For middle schoolers, FCCLA lays a foundation for high school leadership!
