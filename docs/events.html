Fall Leadership


Fall Leadership is a summit held in Pines Manor, Edison. It is an event where New Jersey schools come together to compete in a friendly manner. To win these competitions, it takes hard work, dedication, and skill. Without these traits, one cannot win gold. Our members that represent HHMS are respectful and diligent. We show excellent sportsmanship to the other school even if we get gold or bronze.

Spring Leadership


Spring Leadership is another summit where New Jersey schools compete over a course of two days. This event is held in Cherry Hill, NJ. Once again, our HHMS Spring Leadership representatives are respectful and hardworking. Our resprenties are very aware of their situation, and will act accordingly.
