FCCLA at Herbert Hoover has a huge influence not only on everyone apart of it, but everyone else at the school. FCCLA teaches children at Herbert Hoover Middle School how to be responsible and how to show proper respect for everyone including yourself.

What we do

FCCLA is a pretty much a student run organization here in HHMS. The students make the decisions, the advisors are just there for support.To promote personal growth and leadership development through Family and Consumer Sciences education. Focusing on the multiple roles of family member, wage earner, and community leader, members develop skills for life through: character development, creative and critical thinking, interpersonal communication, practical knowledge, and career preparation.

Our Mission

Our mission at Herbert Hoover, is to make not only our school great, but our entire community. But, the only way this can be accomplished is if all of our members lend a helping hand, in completing all of the tasks we need to. We don’t just make our school a better place to be around and a more fun environment for the faculty and students, we also raise money for different causes in our community and we also make blankets for the women’s shelter. Our mission is to make FCCLA more widely known throughout our community, making it a huge part to not only the member's lives, but everyone else who understands our goal.
