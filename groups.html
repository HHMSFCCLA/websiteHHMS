Nothing Is Particularly Hard if you divide it into small jobs

In FCCLA we have divided ourselves into the following groups to accomplish as much as possible.

Technology

Our Technology Unit is here at FCCLA to help anyone that has any trouble with their Chromebook or even anything that has to do with FCCLA online.

Fundraising

The Fundraising Unit of FCCLA at Herbert Hoover is very important because they make different treats and foods that they sell for a certain price, and all of the money that they receive, will go to a wonderful cause that betters our community. This year at Herbert Hoover, our Fundraising unit has become extremely large and we had to break the unit into two separate groups, each run by a Vice President and Co-Vice President.

Community Service

The Community Service unit loves to give back to their community in any way possible, such as food drives and even making stuff for different shelters.

Coffee Bar

Every Friday, our Coffee Bar unit comes in early, to make breakfast for our amazing faculty, here at Herbert Hoover. Some of the dishes they serve are, Blueberry Parfaits and a variety of Omelettes. Our faculty loves these amazing dishes which makes our Coffee Bar unit one of the busiest teams every week.
